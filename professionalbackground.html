I am 26 years old, and have two beautiful children: a daughter, Spencer Love (6 yrs), and a son, Atticus James (6 mo). I am the first to graduate in my entire immediate family, and am trying to show my children that hard work and dedication will reward those who earn it. Although it has been difficult juggling full-time school, work, and a family to support and take care of, it also shows that I am very proficient at multitasking.


From the time I was 18, I started creating documents with the Adobe Creative Suite. Now, almost a decade later, I am becoming increasingly more adept with the programs through various encounters I have had. Just out of high school I started working on the Priest Lake Visitors Guide project. By the end of the third annual guide I was selling all of the 25K advertising, making advertisements, and designing the entire 40 page guide.


From there, I went to work selling advertising at The Bonner County Daily Bee in Sandpoint, Idaho, as well as Northern Wilds in Grand Marais, Minnesota. The ability to be working amongst designers, editors, advertising representatives, and publishers simultaneously, has shaped my career path. I feel the Technical Communication program through Eastern, is fabulous at packaging a multifaceted degree into a short four years. I intend on graduating, with honors, next year.
